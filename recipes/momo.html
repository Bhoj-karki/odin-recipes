MOMO

Chicken momos are a popular delicacy originating from the Himalayan regions, particularly Nepal and Tibet. These savory dumplings consist of a thin, soft dough filled with a spiced ground chicken mixture. The dough is typically made from all-purpose flour, while the filling features a blend of minced chicken, onions, garlic, ginger, and spices like cumin and coriander. The mixture is wrapped inside the dough, which is then pleated and sealed to form small, bite-sized parcels. Chicken momos are usually steamed, but they can also be fried or pan-fried for a crispy variation. The result is a tender, flavorful dumpling with a juicy filling that bursts with flavor in every bite.

The dipping sauce, often made from roasted tomatoes, garlic, red chilies, and sesame seeds, adds an extra layer of flavor that complements the mildly spiced chicken filling. Chicken momos can be enjoyed as a snack or a meal, offering a perfect balance of textures with the softness of the wrapper and the richness of the filling. Their popularity has spread beyond their regional roots, making them a beloved dish worldwide.
